GDPR Compliance 2018

A Letter to Users

Dear Gary Portal User:

With the May 25 deadline for General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance on the horizon, we are writing to inform you that as of the 18th April 2018, the DEVELOPMENT branches of the Gary Portal applications ARE GDPR compliant.


GDPR BACKGROUND:

The new GDPR laws are possibly the most extensive laws surrounding the protection of data, they are industry disruptive laws that require each individual developer, company, payroll company, and data handler to track each user's individual data and inform each user about the distinct handlings of their personal data, as well as protect it from unwanted acces. As well as this, a developer should provide users the 'right to be forgotten'. This is the right to have your personal data permanently removed from our databases, unless it is essential to the continued running of our business.

GDPR laws are to replace the Data Protection Act of 1998 in the United Kingdom, and are adapted for the current age of technology. There is a greater emphasis on the companies that are entrusted, process and control an individualâ€™s data to have a larger responsibility to protect it. A company can be fined if the systems, companies and external services they use are also not compliant with GDPR.


How we conform to GDPR:

For the DEVELOPMENT branches of the Gary Portal:

  1. You can exercise your 'right to be forgotten' here
  2. You can request for your account to be completely removed here
  3. Your private info is stored on our private database, and no one has access except Head Developers
  4. Our database is supplied by Firebase, from Google LLC - and has built in read/write rules
  5. We do not send any data to external advertisers
  6. We CONFORM to Law Enforcement's or indivdual's requests for personal data.
  7. We DELETE any asset material i.e. GaryGram posts, chats, which break our policies
  8. We require all users to accept to our Policies and GDPR compliance upon signup.

Although this is only currently for our DEVELOPMENT brances, with the roll out of version 3.0.0, these GDPR compliances will come into affect on ALL LIVE versions of the Gary Portal, version 3.0.0 and up.

Please note that if either Firebase or Sendbird roll back their GDPR compliance, we will imemdiately suspend our use of their services, which may result in downtime of our services and online assets.

If you have any questions regarding our GDPR compliance, please contact almurray@gmail.com

If you are on the DEVELOPMENT branch of Gary Portal and do not agree to either our policies or our GPDR compliance, please submit a request for your account to be fully removed.
